Healthy Planet Federation

Healing the planet one person at a time.

Healthy Planet is dedicated to helping Canadians find ways to help reduce their impact on our environment.

As the population of the earth increases, so does our use of fossil fuels. We consume more and more of our natural resources and our actions are affecting the climate.

It is our responsibility to preserve our natural resources and provide future generations with a beautiful, healthy place to live.
