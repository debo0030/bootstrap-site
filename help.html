Conserve Energy

Turning off electronics when they are not being used will save energy. Change your light bulbs to energy efficient ones.

Travel

Carpool, take your bike, the bus or maybe the train. By reducing the number of cars on the road you can cut down on carbon emissions.

Reduce Waste

Reduce what goes into landfill. Try fixing what is broken instead of throwing it away. Purchasing fewer products will reduce the amount of packaging and plastic you need to throw away. Recycle as much as you can.

Conserve Water

Planting a drought resistant garden means you can water less and collecting rain water allows you to further reduce clean water consumption. Turn off the tap when you are not using the water and using an energy saving washer.

Buy Local

Purchase products that are produced locally. Local products do not need to be transported long distances, reducing carbon emissions. Consider growing your own vegetables. Locally grown produce tends to be grown more sustainably.

Renewable Power

Choose renewable power whenever you can. Talk to your power provider to see what percentage of power is produced from renewable sources.
